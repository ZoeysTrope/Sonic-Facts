  • This is the home page for Sonic The Hedgehog facts that I find interesting, and hope you will find interesting too! I have seperated the games into 4 categories. "Classic Games" for all the games that are strictly 2D. "Adventure Games" for all the games released between 1998 and 2006, which the community has dubbed the "Adventure Era". "Modern Games" for games from 2007 up until today, using the modern "Boost Formula", or having one-off game gimmicks, like the parkour in Sonic Lost World. And finally, "Spin-Off Games", which should be easy to understand what's there, Spin-Off titles to the main series that I felt warranted a fact.
